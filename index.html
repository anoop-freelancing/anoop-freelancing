Love the Word of God, Love Christ

Welcome to our home on the web, I trust you will find everything you need to inform you about who we are, what we believe, and how the Lord is leading us as a church body. As you will soon discover, we are a church that loves the Word of God because we love Christ. The purpose for our existence, as we see it, is to proclaim the excellencies of Christ in all things, to the glory of God in the joy of all peoples. The Bible reveals who Jesus is, what Jesus has done to save sinners, who we become when we believe in Jesus, and how to live in a manner that is pleasing to Jesus. Therefore, we emphasize expository preaching, God-centered prayers, Christ-exalting praise, and (by God's grace) committed lives of obedience to our Lord. What excites us as a church is not the latest Christian fads, or programs, but the prospect of what God might do next through a people who are sold out to being ruled by His sufficient word.

Enjoy the site, and I pray that you will have the privilege to join us in one of our services very soon. You are cordially invited.

Pastor Craig Davis, Sr.

We Truly Believe You Can't Out Give God

The Bible says in Matthew 6:24 that the top competitor for our commitment to Jesus is money. Giving allows us to demonstrate that He is more important to us than material things. Not having cash or check on hand doesn’t have to hold you back from giving. There is no organization with more potential to change the world than the local church.

What is Tithing?

A tithe—which just means “tenth”—is defined as the first 10% of a person’s income before taxes or anything else comes out. The Bible says we can take the promises of God to the bank. When we bring our first and best back to God, He promises to bless the rest. We can’t out give God. We know tithing is a huge step of obedience, but you’re not in this alone!
