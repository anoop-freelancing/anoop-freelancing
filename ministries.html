Ministries

Women Ministry

The role of the women in the church is vital to the nurturing, and the overall growth of the church family. Our goal is to ensure that every woman has the opportunity to grow in the knowledge of God, while learning to live productive, spirit-filled lives. We are Christian women working to better ourselves and our church family.

Music Ministry

O, come let us sing unto the Lord: let us make a joyful noise to the rock of our salvation. - Psalm 95:1

At New Mount Zion, our mission is to celebrate the presence of God through singing, dancing, and praising. While praising and worshiping God through song is an integral part of our worship experience, worship truly exists when we fully give ourselves to God with a sincere heart. True worship is a lifestyle!!!

The Music Ministry is comprised of the following:

  • Male Chorus

  • Women’s Chorus

  • Mass Choir

  • Praise and Worship Team

  • Youth Choir (Ages 5 and up)

During worship service at Progressive, we use music and song to help set our minds on God and His might. By doing this together as a congregation, we fulfill another principle of worship - fellowship. Our worship services also incorporate praises through dance, prayer, giving, and hearing the preached word.
